About TrendWire Philippines

Our Mission

TrendWire Philippines is an automated news platform that surfaces and reports on trending topics from across the Philippines. We use AI-powered technology to monitor what Filipinos are talking about — from breaking news and politics to sports, entertainment, and everyday concerns — and deliver well-written, informative articles in real time.

How It Works

  1. Discover. Our system continuously monitors Google Trends and major Philippine news sources to identify what's trending in the country.
  2. Generate. Each trending topic is analyzed and transformed into a comprehensive article using AI, with verified information from multiple sources.
  3. Publish. Articles are reviewed and published on our site, making it easy for readers to stay informed about what matters most to Filipinos.

Our Coverage

We cover a wide range of categories:

  • Disaster — Typhoons, earthquakes, floods, and emergency response
  • Politics — Government, congress, elections, and policy
  • Sports — Basketball, boxing, UAAP, and international competitions
  • Economy — Prices, inflation, business, and markets
  • Health — Public health, diseases, and wellness
  • Technology — Gadgets, AI, apps, and digital trends
  • Entertainment — Movies, music, celebrities, and pop culture
  • General — Everything else Filipinos are talking about

Our Values

We believe in:

  • Accuracy. Every article is grounded in multiple verified sources.
  • Speed. We cover trending topics as they emerge.
  • Relevance. We focus on what matters to Filipino readers.
  • Transparency. We clearly indicate that our content is AI-assisted and sourced from public data.

Technology

TrendWire Philippines is built on modern technology:

  • Frontend: Vanilla JavaScript SPA hosted on GitHub Pages
  • Backend: Supabase (PostgreSQL, Edge Functions)
  • AI: OpenRouter API with multiple language models
  • Data Sources: Google Trends, Rappler, and other RSS feeds
  • Automation: GitHub Actions for scheduled trend fetching
